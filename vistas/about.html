Mexican entrepreneur Francisco LópezSerrano (b. 1998) is best known for his taste and practice in fashion. LópezSerrano spent his childhood in Tepoztlán, Morelos, a tranquil, spiritual place surrounded by nature, an element that notably influences his work. From a young age, LópezSerrano was captivated by textile design, frequently manipulating and intervening in pieces with scissors and threads, experimenting and trying to bring unique pieces to life. His training as an economist plays a fundamental role inS textile creation and experimentation from a logical perspective, coexisting with an experimental and creative viewpoint, converging in collective identity pieces. LópezSerrano does not label himself as an artist or fashion designer; he seeks a balance and harmony between both arts—fashion and painting—aiming to explore the individual intimacy of the observer and their personal relationship with the work. 2024 (PATTERN MAKING, CUTTING AND SEWING, CENTRO DE DISEÑO CINE Y TELEVISIÓN, CDMX)
